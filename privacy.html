Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Stronghold collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Stronghold Quote Tool.

Effective date: 10 April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stronghold (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the Stronghold Quote Tool app.

1. Who this app is for

The Stronghold Quote Tool is intended for authorised users only. It is designed to help approved users generate and manage pricing quotations within the app.

2. Information we collect

We may collect and store the following information:

  • Email address used to sign in or request access
  • Account information needed to manage access to the app
  • Quote data entered by users, including customer names and quote references
  • Saved quote history created within the app
  • Basic technical or usage information needed to operate, secure, and improve the app

3. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide access to the app
  • Authenticate users and manage permissions
  • Generate, save, retrieve, and delete quotes
  • Maintain app performance, reliability, and security
  • Support internal business use of the app

4. Data storage and retention

Data is stored using third-party infrastructure and services that support operation of the app, including cloud database and authentication providers.

Saved quotes are intended to be automatically deleted after 90 days. Other account-related information may be retained for as long as needed to operate the app, manage access, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, database, and authentication providers, where necessary for app functionality.

We may also disclose information where required by law, regulation, legal process, or to protect our rights, users, or business.

6. Data security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect information held within the app. However, no system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

7. User responsibilities

Users should only enter information that is necessary for legitimate business use of the app. Sensitive personal data should not be entered unless strictly necessary and authorised.

8. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be made available on this page with an updated effective date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at sales@stronghold.co.uk or visit www.stronghold.co.uk.
